Let's get started
In this tutorial we will make a real-world GirlFriend app with tales that lets you remember things about your girlfriends. At the end of this tutorial you will not only have learnt tales and made a complete app with it, but also have a brand new application that will help you to astonish your girlfriends by remembering their birthday, their cousin's brother's name, their second brother's daughter's school name, .. Din't I tell you tales was going to be fun?
The app will contain two pages that will look like this
An index page that will list all girlfriends and lets you add one more
An detail page that will let you store notes against a girl friend
